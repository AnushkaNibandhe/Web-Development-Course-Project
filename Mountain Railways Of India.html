Mountain Railways Of India

The Mountain Railways of India are a UNESCO World Heritage Site, consisting of three historic railway lines: Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, Nilgiri Mountain Railway, and Kalka-Shimla Railway. These railways were built during the British era and showcase remarkable engineering in challenging mountainous terrains. They offer scenic journeys through lush hills, tunnels, and bridges. These railways are still operational, preserving their heritage charm with steam and diesel locomotives.
