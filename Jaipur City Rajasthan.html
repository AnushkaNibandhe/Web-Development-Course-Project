Jaipur City, Rajasthan

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the "Pink City" due to its distinct pink-colored buildings. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it is famous for its rich history, grand palaces, and vibrant culture. Key attractions include Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amer Fort, and Jantar Mantar. Jaipur is also part of India's Golden Triangle tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra.
