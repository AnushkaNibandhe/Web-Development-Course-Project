Hill forts of Rajasthan

The Hill Forts of Rajasthan, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, comprise six majestic forts: Chittorgarh, Kumbhalgarh, Ranthambore, Gagron, Amer, and Jaisalmer. Built between the 5th and 18th centuries, they showcase Rajput military architecture, blending defensive structures with palaces, temples, and water reservoirs. These forts symbolize Rajput valor and strategic ingenuity, often set in rugged hill terrains. Their design reflects a unique blend of cultural, historical, and environmental significance.
