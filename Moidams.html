Moidams

Moidams are the royal burial mounds of the Ahom kings and nobles, located mainly in Charaideo, Assam. These mound-like tombs are built using a unique vault system, blending Tai-Ahom and local architectural styles. They serve as an important part of Assam’s cultural heritage and are often compared to the pyramids of Egypt. Many Moidams have been excavated, revealing artifacts and historical treasures. Charaideo Moidams are being considered for UNESCO World Heritage status.
